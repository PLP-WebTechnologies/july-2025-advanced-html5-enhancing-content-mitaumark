About Us

The Big Mamas' Lifestyle is an online platform that aims at uniting all the Milennial out there on Kenya

Started in 2020 by single mothers in Urban Nairobi streets, the organisation has achieved a good reception
countrywide from many women and is enhancing the quality of life to the older women all over Kenya
Our aim is to make lifestyles of the generation titled 'boring' one of the happiest and satisfied generation

Our Vision

  • Provide a platform of free expression for all women through:
    1. Whatsapp Groups
    2. Telegram Channels
    3. Hotline numbers
  • Create a reliable financial lending institution for all our members
    School-fee Aid
    This is fund programme where women can acquuire loans for their
    children in school. The fund sends the required money direct to the schools
    Anza-Biashara Business Fund
    This is special funding programme that helps our members start off personal businesses
  • Offer therapy sessions to our members at subsidised fees to our members
  • Schoolarship offers for vulnerable children from single mothers in country
